Summary

  • No account required. We don’t collect names, emails, or phone numbers.
  • The app works offline (Flashcards & Puzzle). AI recognition uses the camera on-device only; images are not uploaded.
  • Advertising: We use Google AdMob. Ad networks may collect device identifiers and approximate location for ad measurement and fraud prevention.
  • Diagnostics: We may use crash logs and basic analytics to improve stability.

Data We Collect

Directly from you

When you contact support, we receive your message and email address.

Automatically

  • Diagnostics such as crash logs and app performance (via Apple or Firebase Crashlytics if enabled).

How We Use Data

  • To provide core features (e.g., remembering language and settings locally on your device).
  • To show low‑frequency ads and measure their performance.
  • To fix bugs and improve the app.

Sharing

We do not sell personal data. Limited data is shared with service providers strictly to deliver the app (e.g., Google AdMob; Apple for crash reporting).

Your Choices
